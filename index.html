Alongside this, I'm working on smaller projects and individual collaborations under the n/acc banner.

Neo anse's principles are rooted in Human Action, effective acceleration (e/acc), and a strong belief in meritocracy. To learn more, feel free to explore my Thoughts.

Primary Experiment

The first experiment aims to connect 3 to 5 small groups of entrepreneurs or local communities. The goal is to facilitate the posting of offers and requests so they can reach other participants more quickly.

The evaluation will focus on the number of connections established and the acceleration of daily operations. Data collection and experiment validation are planned for the transition between Q1 and Q2. I share all the latest updates about the project's progress in real-time on ùïè #nhbuild.
